Pesto Pizza

Description

Pesto pizza is a traditional Italian Pizza made out of fresh pesto and garnished with some parmesan and cherry tomatoes. Pizza without tomato sauce! Yes, we know there are several abominations in the name of pizza, but trust us when we say this is one of the best pizza you'll ever taste'. You'll thank us later.'

While pizza traditionally has tomato sauce (with the notable exception of Pizza Bianca), pesto just gives the dish a unique kick, be it from the freshness of herbs or the nuttiness of pinenuts.

Ingredients

  • 500 gm Refined Wheat Flour
  • 400 ml Water
  • a pinch of Salt
  • Sourdough starter / yeast
  • Basil
  • Green chillies
  • Parsley
  • Cheese
  • 200 gm Cherry Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Mix flour with water, sugar and salt overnight.
  2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Hand stretch the pizza dough to 12 inches.
  3. Spread pesto on pizza crust. Top with tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, red onions, artichoke hearts and feta cheese.
  4. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned.
